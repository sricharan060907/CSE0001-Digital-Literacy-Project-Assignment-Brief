Stay Safe Online Prevention Checklist

Never Share Your UPI PIN OTP or Banking Details.
Never share UPI PIN when receiving payments.
Avoid Unknown Links and Unverified Apps.
Check Who Is Calling When They Say They Are From Your Bank or Company.
Enable App Lock and Two-Factor Authentication for Financial Apps.
Review Bank Statements Often.
A stranger's random QR code might hide a trap. Scanning it could open doors you did not expect. Unknown links often lead somewhere risky. A quick click may cause long problems. Hidden data waits behind those pixels. You cannot see what lies beneath. Better to ignore than regret later.
Install apps only from official stores such as Play Store or App Store.
Always confirm the recipient's name when using UPI.
Report unauthorised UPI transactions right away.
