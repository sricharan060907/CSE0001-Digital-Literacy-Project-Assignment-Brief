It struck me during my look into online crime just how basic some scams really are - especially the ones around UPI fraud. Not everyone who gets caught is reckless; often, a well-timed message or call pushes them off balance. These alerts play on worry or pressure, making quick decisions feel unavoidable. What became clear was that knowing a few core habits could make all the difference - even for those with degrees. Awareness, it turns out, doesn’t always follow education.

Because of this, I’m choosing to rethink how careful I am during online purchases. Every request will be checked carefully before I respond, while personal details stay protected. Sharing what I learn with loved ones becomes part of the routine - keeping them informed helps keep them secure. Staying alert turns out to matter most when it comes to staying safe online.
